About Us

The parent firm Newborn has been in the baby clothing manufacturing business for five years, and they have now made the descision to form a new company in order to diversify their product offering. Neobaby is the new business, and it will produce diapers and market them to customers at fair prices.

Vision

“ Be the leading baby-centered company, where customers can find affordable, quality baby diapers.”
